Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Ykjam Terjime respects your privacy and gives you full control over your data.

Quick summary: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not store any user data on our servers. To delete your data, simply clear app data or uninstall the app from your device.

What Data Does Ykjam Terjime Store?

Ykjam Terjime stores the following information only on your device (never on our servers):

  • Favorites — phrases you marked as favorite
  • Settings — language preferences, audio settings
  • Statistics — local usage tracking (which phrases you viewed, etc.)
  • Search history — recent searches

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information (names, emails, phone numbers) on remote servers.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Clear App Data from Inside the App

  1. Open Ykjam Terjime
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Clear History or Reset All Settings
  4. Confirm to delete all local data

Option 2: Clear App Data from Device Settings (Android)

  1. Open device Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Ykjam Terjime
  3. Tap Storage & cache
  4. Tap Clear storage (and Clear cache)

Option 3: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Ykjam Terjime from your device automatically removes all locally stored data. There is no remaining data on our servers because we don't store any.

Third-Party Services

If you used the AI Assistant or Translation features (when available), your text queries are processed by third-party services (Google Gemini, MyMemory, LibreTranslate). We do not control or store this data. To request data deletion from these services, please contact them directly:

  • Google Gemini: Google Account Help
  • MyMemory Translation: MyMemory Privacy
  • LibreTranslate: LibreTranslate Site

What We Do With Deletion Requests

Since we do not store any user data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete on our side. However, we are happy to:

  • Confirm in writing that we hold no data about you
  • Help you remove app data from your device
  • Direct you to third-party services where applicable
